Aircraft Encyclopedia

The aircraft type encyclopedia of flugzeuginfo.net contains the most important facts and figures of most civil and military aircraft built in history and today.

Until today a total number of 1258 different airplanes and helicopters were included in the database. And the encyclopedia is still growing with some hundreds more to come.

The range of aircraft starts with the very early ones like the Wright Flyer and goes to the later Spad or Sopwith bi-planes from the era of the First World War or their competitors built from Fokker. It includes the first jet fighter aircraft like the Messerschmitt Me 262 and the Gloster Meteor. Furthermore you can find here information about the early jet airliner like the Boeing 707 or the spy planes as for example the U-2 and the SR-71. Facts and figures of the Airbus A320 family and the long production Boeing 737 are contained as also airliner from the Soviet Union like the Tu-154. Current military aircraft like the F-22 or the Eurofighter as also current production airliner like the Boeing 777 and the Airbus A380 complete this huge database.

Each aircraft page includes at least one downloadable photo and the technical specifications. This encyclopedia is constantly updated. Please be aware that all photos are copyright protected - no usage allowed without proper permission.

Flugzeuginfo.net

The web portal flugzeuginfo.net includes a comprehensive civil and military aircraft encyclopedia. It provides code tables for aerodromes, air operators including the world's major airlines and for ICAO and IATA codes for aircraft. The website has also a photo gallery and gives you an overview of all aviation museums worldwide.
